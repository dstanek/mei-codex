**Email Subject Line**
- @Notebook - to put a note directly into a notebook
- #tag - to tag a note with an existing tag
- !reminder
	- !12/25/2015 
	- !tomorrow

Order of operations: [Title of Note] ![Reminder Date] @[Folder] #[Tag]

**Searching**
- **_created:day-5_** _-_ to view notes created in the last 5 days
- **_resource:audio/*_** - to find notes with Audio attachments
- **_source:mobile.android_** _-_ to view notes that were Created from a Android Device

There are lots of other interesting things to do with the [search grammar](https://dev.evernote.com/doc/articles/search_grammar.php)
