Note types:
* Fleeting
* Reference
* Literature
* Permanent

## Reference Notes

Highlights and direct quotes that help us write Literature notes. Notes from Readwise and AI are examples of reference notes.

```yaml
type: reference
```
## Literature Notes

These are notes in my own words about a reference note or things I am reading. Stick to the most important ideas and be concise.

```yaml
type: literature
```

## Permanent Notes

Permanent notes are my own thoughts/ideas. They are possibly linked to other notes, but will hold only a single idea. The should be complete and concise.
