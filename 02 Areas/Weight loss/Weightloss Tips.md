## Nutrition
- Reduce carbs to 50g daily and replace with protein and healthy fats
- Eliminate snacking between meals
- 18/6 intermittent fasting (noon-6pm window)
- Protein intake: .7-1g per pound of goal body weight
- Protein first at every meal

## Exercise
- Perform interval sprint training 3 times weekly (20s on, 90s off, 3 rounds)
- Walk 10k+ steps daily (15-20 minute brisk walk after meals)

## Sleeping
- 90 minutes deep sleep nightly
- banana tea - boil whole banana, without the ends, for 5 minutes
- cold bedroom (65 degrees)
- complete darkness (sleep mask)
- mouth taping
- 400-600 mg magnesium glycinate 30 minutes before sleep
